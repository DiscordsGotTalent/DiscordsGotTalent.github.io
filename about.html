What is DGT?

DGT is a talent server on Discord currently owned by Dino, Chelsie, and Mystic. But not only is it a talent based Discord server, it is also an event server. Events are hosted by event leaders, or hosts. We have events such as gamenights, karaokes, singing competitions, and much more!

We are planning on hosting golden competitions in the future. If you don't know what that is, just wait and see in the future!
